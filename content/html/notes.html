Shakti Chattopadhaya is enfant terrible in rich heritage of Bengali poetry.

His angry refusal of urban hypocrisy had resonated with urban youth, especially the rebels. However, lyrical quality of his poems -- deeply rooted in beauty of rural Bengal -- had surpassed angst in his writings.

As a homage to this great poet, this is a collection of his poems translated for international lovers of poetry.
These poems, and few more, are available in printed and electronic form at Amazon website.
