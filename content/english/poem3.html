Oboni, Are you home?

Neighborhood asleep
Doors shut tight
Night's sudden knock:
"Oboni, are you home?"

Pours here 'round the year
Cattle clouds roam
Dejected tall green grass
Grabs the door
"Oboni, are you home?"

Faint latent pain in heart
I drowse
Sudden knock of the dark:
"Oboni, are you home?"
